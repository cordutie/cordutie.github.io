About me

Hi! I’m Esteban, a predoctoral researcher at the Music Technology Group (MTG) in Barcelona. My research explores sound representations for conditioned sound generation under the supervision of Dr. Xavier Serra and Dr. Frederic Font, where I work closely with Dr. Lonce Wyse and the Freesound team. If you’re interested in collaborating, feel free to get in touch :)
